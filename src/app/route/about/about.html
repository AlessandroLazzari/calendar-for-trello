about w11k Trello Calendar

Trello is a productivity tool that helps you to organize projects by creating individual lists, boards and cards. Manage your projects in teams, get more flexible and improve your workflow.

The w11k Trello Calendar allows you to organize all cards of all boards in one calendar. Trello Calendar is Open Source and available at GitHub.

Feature Requests

This Trello Calendar is under heavy development. Feel free to submit feature requests or bug reports to our GitHub Repository or directly to w11k GmbH.
